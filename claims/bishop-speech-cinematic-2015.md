The official upload's own description reads "Opening cinematic for Squadron 42. As seen
during CitizenCon 2015." — verified in-browser 2026-07-04 (channel: Star Citizen, published
Oct 10, 2015, 4:13, ~701K views). Speech content facts verifiable from the video itself:
Bishop names Tiber, Orion, Caliban, and Virgil as abandoned systems, invokes the Vega
tragedy, and closes on victory/survival. The speech proper runs ~1 minute 20 seconds.

ground.", "…without victory there can be no survival!") — verified verbatim against the
video's transcript 2026-07-04. Do not expand to long verbatim excerpts without Doc's
explicit sign-off.
