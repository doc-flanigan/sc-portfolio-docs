Key KB wording (fetched 2026-07-12): aUEC is "a temporary currency specifically designed for
Alpha testing"; "there is every chance your balance will change or reset as development
continues"; "loss or gains of aUEC will not affect your UEC total when the game moves out of
testing into Live release"; patch notes "will indicate whether previous aUEC values will
carry over (Long Term Persistence enabled means aUEC should not be wiped)"; "in general, we
will try to avoid wiping out" progress. Pledge-store items (game packages, ships bought with
money) are account-level and are restored after any wipe. Do NOT publish specific future
wipe dates as fact — whether a given patch (e.g. 4.9) wipes is known only when its patch
notes say so; attribute predictions to community expectation.
