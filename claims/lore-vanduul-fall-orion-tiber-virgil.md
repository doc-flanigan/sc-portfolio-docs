Verified against the full Galactic Guide bodies (comm-links 13061, 13614, 14437) via
api.star-citizen.wiki. Details per guide: Orion — Kingship arrived Feb 16, 2712 through a
jump point later coded VS-09 "Vulture"; a 2686 clan attack had earlier been repelled.
Tiber — Siege of Tiber lasted four years, 2732–2736; nickname "Grinder"; nineteen failed
UEE initiatives to retake it since. Virgil — after Tiber cracked, the Navy could not mount
an effective defense; the Vanduul bombed Virgil I into permanent nuclear winter (Squadron
214 rescue stand is also from this guide). All dates are in-universe lore dates — frame as
lore, not real-world history.
