Added 2026-07-15. Myth entry (present-tense claim) — canon companion: [[base-building-citizencon-2954-not-yet-live]]. Base building was unveiled at CitizenCon 2954 ("Crafting Your Home", Oct 2024) but has not shipped as of Alpha 4.8 (July 2026): Zyloh-CIG's Nov 2024 Pioneer update says details will come "as we approach the launch of base building," and the May 2026 Star Citizen Live vehicle Q&A still discusses base building as upcoming. Crafting T0 (Alpha 4.7) covers personal FPS armor/weapons only — not structures. Verify against the cited source(s) before re-use.
