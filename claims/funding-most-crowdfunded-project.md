The $1B figure itself is verified (see funding-one-billion-may-2026), but the comparative
superlative ("most crowdfunded in history") is not assertable from CIG sources alone — CIG
does not publish comparisons, and ranking requires third-party data outside the allowed
source set. Accepted site wording: state the verified $1B fact and let the comparison ride
on it ("no other crowdfunded project comes close" is editorial). Flag any copy that cites a
third-party ranking as the source.
