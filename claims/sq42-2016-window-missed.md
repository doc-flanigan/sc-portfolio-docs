The October 2016 Monthly Studio Report (15587) states in writing the team "didn't wind up
being able to show off" its SQ42 efforts at CitizenCon 2946. The November 2016 anniversary
Letter from the Chairman (15603) articulates the no-hard-dates stance. The 2016 window
itself was promoted at CitizenCon 2015 (livestream replay comm-link 15002) and on the RSI
SQ42 page of that era — the window's promotion is corroborated but no longer visible on a
live official page; cite the aftermath comm-links, not a dead page.
