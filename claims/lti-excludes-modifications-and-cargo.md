Added 2026-07-14. Verify against the cited source(s) before re-use.

Verbatim source text (comm-link mirror, wiki id 14282, "Insurance FAQ", original page
https://robertsspaceindustries.com/en/faq/Insurance-FAQ — now 302s to
support.robertsspaceindustries.com root, page likely folded into the Zendesk KB):
"Lifetime insurance does not exist for modifications or cargo." Cross-confirmed by the 2012
comm-link (id 12820, "Insurance FAQ and Update"): "Pilots in Star Citizen can purchase
insurance policies for their ships, modifications and cargo" — i.e. modifications/cargo are
separately insurable (risk-rated), LTI only ever applied to the hull. Both LTI and Standard
Hull Insurance texts read identically: "Replaces your ship hull... Hull is replaced with an
identical model in equivalent condition" — hull only, no mention of loadout/paint state.
