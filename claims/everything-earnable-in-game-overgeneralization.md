Added 2026-07-15. Visitor claim was the absolute 'everything will be buyable in game, eventually.' CIG's own August 2023 Subscriber Promotions comm-link (19385) states the goal explicitly: 'Our goal is to make most items earnable in-game' — many Subscriber Flair items go into the in-game loot table after a three-month exclusivity window, but 'some exclusive digital event items, such as those from CitizenCon Goodies Packs, will not be discoverable via the in-game loot s[ystem].' The Anvil Odin comm-link (21133) restates the standard ship policy ('These ships will be available for in-game credits and/or will be otherwise earnable through play') while also selling five Odin Founders Club paints only via a one-time Warbond concept sale not obtainable with store credit — an ambiguous case for cosmetics, not a stated permanent 'never available in-game' exclusion in the comm-link body itself. Net: true for ships (see ships-earnable-in-game-auec), but the word 'everything' overclaims for cosmetics/flair/digital-event items, where CIG's own language is 'most,' with acknowledged carve-outs. Rephrased as verified: most items become earnable in-game, not literally all.
