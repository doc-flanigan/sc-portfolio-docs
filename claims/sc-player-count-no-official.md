Absence-of-publication phrasing (same pattern as [[no-official-coupon-codes]]): the claim is
that no official stat EXISTS, not that the game is small or large. The funding-goals page is
JS-rendered — verify the accounts counter in a browser. Third-party estimate sites
(activeplayer.io, playerauctions, mmo-population) disagree by 5x (≈13K vs ≈63K) and none
disclose methodology — cite them only as examples of disagreement, never as data. Real
verifiable signals to offer instead: registered accounts (funding tracker), funding rate,
server capacity per shard. Related: [[funding-one-billion-may-2026]] (6.5M backer accounts,
backers ≠ paying players — keep the same phrasing discipline).
