- Document header: "LAST UPDATED AND EFFECTIVE AS OF SEPTEMBER 28, 2023."
- Section: **VII. Store Credits, Exchanges and Gifting — A. Store Credits**. Quote: "Store
  Credits obtained by converting Pledge Items are not refundable in real life currency."
  Store Credits "may subsequently use... to acquire certain Pledge Items which is the only
  permitted use of Store Credits."
- Site copy check: StarCitizenHelp's and dayonecitizen's glossary.ts both define "Store
  Credit" as *"Real-money credit on your RSI account... refunds back to your account, never
  expires"* — the phrase "Real-money credit" conflicts with the ToS ("not refundable in real
  life currency") and should be softened; flagged as NEEDS REPHRASE in the 2026-07-23 audit.
  The adjacent "Melt" glossary entry in the same files ("Unlike a real refund, melting is
  instant and the credit only spends inside the RSI store") is correct and not in conflict.
