# GEO Content Audit — 2026-07-05

Network-wide scan of 84 pages across 10 sites for citation-worthiness signals:
extractable answer paragraphs (ANS), FAQPage JSON-LD, other schema, Sources
sections (claims ledger), llms.txt coverage, prose volume, and metadata quality.
Scanner: static analysis of each `page.tsx` + one-level component/data imports;
suspicious flags hand-verified before inclusion. StarCitizenHelp excluded
(sunset — no investment). 42ndsquadron included informationally (pre-launch).

**The playbook this audits against** (proven on freeflyevent, then bestspacesim
2026-07-05: 776 citations, 36% share on "is star citizen worth it"):
1. Direct extractable answer paragraph above the fold (bold verdict/answer)
2. FAQPage JSON-LD with real question-phrased queries
3. Sources section from the claims ledger (PageSources) — GEO trust signal
4. Listed in llms.txt with a descriptive line
5. Honest metadata; schema matched to page type (HowTo/DefinedTerm/Event/Review)
